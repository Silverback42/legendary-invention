## Description

Schlicht is the budget app for people who like it simple. No complicated features, no learning curve – just what you actually need to stay on top of your spending.

**What makes Schlicht different:**

• Log an expense in under 3 seconds – so fast it becomes a habit
• Clean dashboard with donut chart and categories at a glance
• Monthly budgets per category – see instantly where you stand
• Month-over-month comparison – spot trends in your spending
• Share your monthly summary – show friends your progress (percentages only, no amounts!)

**Private & Offline:**

Schlicht stores everything locally on your device. No account needed, no tracking, no cloud required. Your financial data belongs to you – and only you.

**Who is Schlicht for?**

Whether you're a student, career starter, couple, or family – Schlicht adapts with personalized categories and budget suggestions that fit your lifestyle.
