## 2. Core Principle: Your Data Stays With You

Schlicht is an offline app. All financial data (expenses, categories, budgets) is stored exclusively on your device. **No** data is uploaded to any server.

## 3. What Data Is Processed?

### 3.1 Locally Stored Data
- Expenses (amount, category, date, note)
- Categories (name, icon, color)
- Budgets (amount per category and month)
- Settings (currency, language, input mode)

This data never leaves your device.

### 3.2 Subscription Management (RevenueCat)
If you purchase a Premium subscription, payment is processed through Apple App Store / Google Play Store. For subscription management, we use RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc., USA). RevenueCat receives:
- An anonymous app user ID
- Purchase receipts (from Apple/Google)
- Device type and operating system

RevenueCat does **not** receive any financial data from the app. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### 3.3 No Analytics, No Tracking
Schlicht does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or crash reporting. We do not know who you are or how you use the app.

## 4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

- Local data storage: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance – the app requires local data to function)
- RevenueCat: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance – subscription management)

## 5. Data Sharing

Your financial data is shared with **no one**. The only data transmission occurs through subscription management via RevenueCat (see 3.2).

## 6. Data Retention

Local data is stored until you delete it (Settings → "Clear all data") or uninstall the app.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights at any time.
